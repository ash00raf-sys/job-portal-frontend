Railway Recruitment 2026 is expected to offer thousands of job opportunities across India through the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB). Candidates looking for secure government jobs can apply for various posts including Group D, NTPC, and Junior Engineer roles.

This guide covers the latest railway job notifications, eligibility, salary details, and how to apply online.

## Why Choose Railway Jobs in 2026

Railway jobs are among the most preferred government jobs due to:

- High job security  
- Attractive salary and allowances  
- Free or discounted railway travel  
- Pension and retirement benefits  
- Work-life balance

## Railway Recruitment 2026 – Key Posts

### RRB Group D Jobs

Group D recruitment is one of the largest hiring drives in India.

**Posts Include:**

- Track Maintainer  
- Helper / Assistant  
- Porter  

### RRB NTPC Jobs

Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) include clerical and administrative roles.

**Popular Posts:**

- Station Master  
- Goods Guard  
- Junior Clerk  
- Commercial Apprentice  

### RRB Junior Engineer (JE)

Technical candidates can apply for Junior Engineer posts.

**Fields Include:**

- Civil Engineering  
- Mechanical Engineering  
- Electrical Engineering

## Eligibility Criteria for Railway Jobs

### Educational Qualification

- Group D: 10th pass / ITI  
- NTPC: 12th pass / Graduation  
- JE: Engineering Diploma / Degree  

### Age Limit

- Minimum: 18 years  
- Maximum: 33 years (varies by post)  

Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules.

## Railway Salary Structure

Railway jobs offer competitive salaries along with benefits.

**Approximate Salary:**

- Group D: ₹22,000 – ₹35,000  
- NTPC: ₹25,000 – ₹50,000  
- JE: ₹35,000 – ₹70,000  

Additional perks include travel allowance, housing benefits, and medical facilities.

## How to Apply for Railway Recruitment 2026

Follow these steps:

1. Visit the official RRB website  
2. Select your regional RRB zone  
3. Register and fill the application form  
4. Upload required documents  
5. Pay the application fee  
6. Submit the form
