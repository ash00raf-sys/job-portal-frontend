## Key Developments

Global logistics company Aramex has announced fresh hiring for Warehouse Assistant roles, opening new employment opportunities in the fast-growing logistics and supply chain sector.

The recruitment comes at a time when demand for warehouse and delivery operations is increasing due to the rapid expansion of e-commerce and global shipping networks. Industry experts say logistics companies are actively strengthening their workforce to manage rising volumes of goods movement.

According to job details available on Apply on Aramex Careers, the Warehouse Assistant role involves handling daily warehouse operations such as receiving shipments, organizing inventory, and dispatching goods efficiently.

## Key Responsibilities

Receive and inspect incoming goods

Maintain inventory and stock records

Organize and store materials properly

Assist in packing and dispatch operations

Ensure warehouse safety and cleanliness

## Requirements

Candidates applying for the role are generally expected to have

Basic warehouse or logistics experience (preferred)

Ability to handle physical tasks and lifting

Basic communication skills

Attention to detail and teamwork ability

Similar warehouse roles in the UAE indicate that candidates must also follow safety procedures and maintain organized stock systems as part of daily operations.

## Growing Demand in Logistics Jobs

The logistics sector is witnessing strong hiring growth globally, with companies expanding operations across multiple countries. Aramex operates in over 65 countries and continues to invest in workforce expansion and operational efficiency.

Experts highlight that warehouse jobs are becoming one of the most accessible entry points for job seekers, including freshers and candidates looking for overseas opportunities.

## How to Apply

Interested candidates can apply directly through the official careers portal: [apply here](https://careers.aramex.com/job/Warehouse-Assistant/7900-en_US)

Applicants are advised to apply early as positions may fill quickly due to high demand.
