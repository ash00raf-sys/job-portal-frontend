## Job Overview

Kerala PSC invites online applications from Ex-servicemen belonging to the Scheduled Caste Converted to Christianity (SCCC) communities for appointment as Security Guard/Security Guard Grade II/Watcher Grade II in various government-owned companies/corporations/boards. One vacancy under Category No. 18/2026.

- **Organization:** Kerala Public Service Commission
- **Post Name:** Security Guard/ Security Guard GradeII / Watcher Grade II
- **Location:** Various Govt Owned Companies /Corporations/ Boards
- **Total Vacancies:** 01(One)
- **Application Mode:** Online only through One Time Registration on www.keralapsc.gov.in
- **Last Date:** 29.04.2026 Wednesday up to 12 midnight.

## How to Apply

Apply online only via One Time Registration at www.keralapsc.gov.in. Registered candidates should login to their profile and click Apply Now for the post, upload photograph as per instructions, and submit the application before the last date. No application fee. Keep a printout/soft copy of the submitted application.
